Biography

I am an Associate Professror at Guangxi University of Finance and Economics. I received Ph.D. degree and Master degree from School of Information Engineering, Wuhan University of Technology in 2016 and 2009 respectively (advised by Prof. Quan Liu), and B.E degree from College of Physical Science and Technology, Guangxi Normal University in 2002. I carried out scientific research as a visiting scholar in South-Central University for Nationalities, Wuhan University and University of Technology, Sydney. I have authored more than 20 papers in refereed international journals and conference proceedings, including ACM MM, Information Sciences, CGF, Neurocomputing and Chinese Journal of Electronics etc. My research interests include image and video editing, computer graphics and signal processing.
